Our Story

Built on Precision.
Driven by Trust.

From a single factory in Taiwan to a global fastening partner trusted by leading brands across five continents — this is the story of Loyal & Birch Co.

Where It Began

A Quarter Century
of Craftsmanship.

Our journey began in 1999 under the name TAIKYU, with a singular focus: introducing Japan's most advanced complex coating technology to Taiwan's fastener industry. From day one, the benchmark was set not by local standards, but by the world's most demanding markets.

In 2010, we formally established Loyal & Birch Co. — a brand built on the manufacturing expertise we had spent a decade perfecting. That same year, we earned the trust of global power tool leaders including Hitachi, Makita, and Max, supplying the precision fasteners that power their products.

Today

Global Reach.
Taiwan Roots.

Today, Loyal & Birch Co. operates five production plants and serves customers across Europe, North America, Japan, Australia, and Southeast Asia. Our product range spans construction, AI server infrastructure, and automotive applications — and we continue to push into the next frontier of precision manufacturing.

Our Vision

To be the world's most trusted OEM fastening partner — combining Taiwan's manufacturing heritage with global innovation ambition.
